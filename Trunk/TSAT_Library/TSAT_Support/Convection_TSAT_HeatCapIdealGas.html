TSAT: Ideal Gas Heat Capacity Library Block

Reference for the information in this help file: Cengal, Y.A., and Boles, M.A., Thermodynamics: An Engineering Approach 7th Ed., McGraw-Hill, New York, NY, 2011. Print.

This block estimates the heat capacity of and ideal gas using the following cubic polynomial fit:

$$ C_{p} = a + bT + cT^{2} + dT^{3} $$

where a, b, c, and d are constants dependent on the gas. Constants for some common gases are provided in the following table:
